JK Crew Conversion

Overview

The JK Crew started as a concept vehicle engineered in the imagination and heart of our team. The design is inspired from the desire of the Jeep community for a Jeep pickup. What started as dream began taking shape on paper and slowly became a truly iconic vehicle capturing the spirit of rugged exploration.

Vehicle Design

Forged with form and function in mind, the JK Crew performs as comfortable off-road as on. A 5'x4'-6"' bed is added to a standard four-door Jeep Wrangler for increased carrying capacity. The custom fabricated bed design is ready to go anywhere with intentional descent angle clearance.
