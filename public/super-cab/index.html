Super Cab Conversion

Overview

The Bruiser Super Cab combines the capabilities of the 2-door Jeep Wrangler and the Bruiser JK Crew, providing a nimbler vehicle to conquer your terrain and provides the cargo carrying benefits of a truck bed.

Vehicle Design

The Super Cab is equipped with a 550 HP Bruiser Conversions LS3, Atlas 3.0 transfer case, a Currie Enterprises short arm suspension, and Ultimate Dana 60 axles. It is equipped with a 5'x4'-6"' utility bed and a high departure angle frame with a full width HDPE skid plate. Access to the integrated 2” Reese receiver is gained using the flip-up license plate bracket, which also folds up to avoid damage while driving off road. The exterior features Rigid Industries LED lighting, a Savvy Offroad front bumper, Warn Zeon 10s winch with Factor55 Flat-Link, GenRight Off Road rocker guards, and TnT Customs aluminum belly skid plates. The custom soft top maintains the factory “SunRider” option and is easily removable if you desire to do so.
